About Me

I got into coding after I discovered Codecademya few years ago, which quickly resulted in programming becoming a hobby. It was interesting for me because I was always curious how the software I used in my daily life worked and was made. I eventually chose to do an IT course at college as I was interested in doing software engineering at university. As I was about to do my last year I made the decision to do an apprenticeship, as I wanted something more practical which eventually led me to the GA bootcamp. The bootcamp greatly helped me in gaining skills that I needed to convert my coding hobby into a career. I am now looking for a role as a Junior Developer where I can connect with like minded people and grow my skills.
